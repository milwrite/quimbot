Anchor moment one: paraphrase the post and its situation. Never reproduce more than ~5 consecutive words of original text. Describe what the poster needed, what they posted, and how the thread responded. 80–120 words.

Anchor moment two may use a second post that shows the year's work in a different register or campus. 80-120 words.

Closing paragraph should name this year's specific expression of the throughline and link forward or backward to an adjacent year. 50-80 words.
