5 Questions to Ask the Boss

These five questions resolve the biggest unknowns in the current Mobile Lab billing proof of concept.

  1. Question 1
    How do you currently count billable units per case — per cassette, per specimen, or per case?
    This decides how claims are assembled and how CPT stacking works.
  2. Question 2
    Which payers require split billing for technical and professional components, and which ones allow global billing?
    This controls claim structure, modifier use, and whether one encounter becomes one claim or two.
  3. Question 3
    What denial reasons show up most often right now?
    The most common denial codes tell us which automation pathway to build first.
  4. Question 4
    At what dollar amount do you stop chasing a claim and write it off, and does that threshold change by payer type?
    This defines the collections branch and the write-off decision rule.
  5. Question 5
    Where does adjudication data come from today — clearinghouse, 835 files, manual EOB posting, or an outsourced biller?
    This tells us what data actually exists to drive automation after submission.
