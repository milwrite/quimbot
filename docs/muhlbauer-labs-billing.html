Open Gaps — Resolution Required
Gap 01 Specimen Grouping Rule Bill per cassette, per specimen, or per case? 88305 stacking behavior varies by payer contract and must be defined before automated claim assembly.
Gap 02 TC / PC Split Policy Define whether professional and technical components are split-billed or global-billed per payer. Drives modifier usage and claim structure.
Gap 03 Medicare / TRICARE LCD Lookup Which dermpath CPT codes require prior authorization under active LCDs? Need real-time NCD/LCD lookup integrated into claim submission gate.
Gap 04 Write-off Dollar Threshold Define the balance floor for write-off vs. collections escalation. Should vary by payer type (Medicare, commercial, self-pay) and balance band.
Gap 05 Referring Provider Loop Should results and billing close auto-report back to the referring dermatologist's EHR on claim resolution? Defines integration scope.
