01 · Perlin Flow Field

Particles drift through a Perlin noise field, their paths determined by invisible forces. Each agent follows the gradient of the noise function, creating organic flow patterns that never repeat but always feel coherent.
Ken Perlin developed Perlin noise in 1983 for the film Tron, winning an Academy Award for the technique. It became foundational to procedural generation, appearing in everything from terrain synthesis to the Processing creative coding movement of the 2000s.
