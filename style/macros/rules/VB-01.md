# VB-01 — Verb audit

**Category:** Verb  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** Yes (all profiles)

---

## Rule

For every sentence in a revision pass, ask: is this the most descriptively precise and rhetorically effective verb available? Weak copula constructions and passive nominalizations almost always have a stronger replacement. Find the verb that carries the meaning rather than outsourcing it to an adjective or a participle.

---

## Diagnostic questions

1. Is the verb a form of "be"? If so, look for the real action hiding in an adjective or a following noun.
2. Is the verb passive + past participle? Name the agent and make the verb active.
3. Does the verb outsource meaning to an adverb? ("greatly increased" → what is the precise verb for that degree of increase?)
4. Is the verb one of the banned jargon verbs? Replace.

---

## Banned verb list

| Banned | Use instead |
|---|---|
| utilize | use |
| leverage | use, pull from, draw on |
| facilitate | enable, allow, help, let |
| implement | build, run, set up, install |
| enhance | improve, sharpen, extend |
| optimize | tune, adjust, refine |
| foster | build, encourage, support |
| drive (business sense) | push, pull, cause, create |
| empower | let, allow, give — or just name the action |
| incentivize | reward, push, pay |

---

## Examples

**Bad:**
> Participation trajectories are platform-mediated outcomes.

> The infrastructure is shaped by competing interests.

> The data is utilized to generate synthetic examples.

**Good:**
> Participation trajectories encode the pressures of the infrastructure they run through.

> Competing interests shaped the infrastructure.

> The pipeline uses the data to generate synthetic examples.

---

## When the copula is correct

"The cloze procedure is a fill-in-the-blank task" — acceptable. The copula here defines; the noun phrase after "is" does real semantic work as a classification. The test: does the "be" verb carry meaning as a definition or equation? If yes, keep it. If it's a placeholder for a real action, replace it.
