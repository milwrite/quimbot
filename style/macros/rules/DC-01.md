# DC-01 — AI slop ban

**Category:** Diction  
**Added:** foundational  
**Non-negotiable:** Yes (all profiles)

---

## Banned words (full list)

delve, tapestry, vibrant, crucial, comprehensive, meticulous, embark, robust, seamless, groundbreaking, synergy, transformative, paramount, multifaceted, myriad, cornerstone, reimagine, empower, catalyst, invaluable, bustling, nestled, realm, foster, drive (business jargon sense), utilize, leverage, facilitate, implement, enhance, optimize

---

## Pattern test

If the word could be replaced with a plainer Anglo-Saxon verb or noun and the sentence improves, replace it.

"Utilize" → "use"  
"Facilitate" → "run," "help," "make possible" (whichever is precise)  
"Leverage" → "use," "build on," "draw from"  

---

## What replaces them

Use: build, cut, break, run, ship, fix, test, push, pull, grab, drop, hit, show, reveal, compel, generate, drive (physical sense only)

---

## Note on "crucial"

"Crucial" and "important" are almost never needed. If the thing is important, the sentence that describes it makes it important. If the sentence doesn't, the adjective won't save it.
