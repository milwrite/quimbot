# CL-02 — Bound the claim

**Category:** Claim  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** No (revision habit — enforced via CL-01)

---

## Rule

Scope every claim to what the evidence actually shows. No claim should extend past the observation that grounds it. Name the specific source of the claim in the same sentence or the immediately preceding one.

---

## Diagnostic

Ask: what evidence supports this claim? Is that evidence named in the sentence or the one before? Does the claim's scope match the evidence's scope?

---

## Examples

**Bad:**
> This shows that all online platforms cooperate with law enforcement.

> The data reveals how universities have responded to surveillance pressures.

**Good:**
> The compliance record shows that Reddit cooperated with law enforcement in the period 2012–2022.

> The 847 documents released under FOIA show how CUNY handled requests in that five-year window.

---

## Bounding vocabulary

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---|---|
| "this shows that" | "the record shows that," "the data show that" (name the source) |
| "all platforms" | "Reddit," "the platform" (name the instance) |
| "universities" | "CUNY," "the institutions in this dataset" |
| "reveals how X has" | "reveals how X did [in period Y]" |
