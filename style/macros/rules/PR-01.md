# PR-01 — Sentence length variation

**Category:** Prose rhythm  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** No (revision habit)

---

## Rule

Mix short and long sentences. Three or more consecutive sentences of similar length reads as a metronome. A short sentence after a long one lands harder; a long sentence after a short one extends the idea. Vary on purpose.

---

## Diagnostic

Read three consecutive sentences and count words. Are they all similar in length (within 5 words of each other)? Vary.

---

## Patterns to use

**Long → Short (emphasis):**
> The ranking algorithm sorts posts by vote score and recency, burying contributions that arrive after the early engagement window regardless of their quality or relevance to the thread. Removal is not erasure.

**Short → Long (extension):**
> The record is clear. Reddit's transparency reports show compliance rates rising from 88% in 2012 to 96% in 2022, a pattern consistent with voluntary cooperation that long predates the platform's public statements about user privacy.

**Fragment for emphasis (use sparingly):**
> Three mechanisms shape every interaction: ranking, retention, logging. Not one of them is visible to the user.

---

## Sentence ceiling

No sentence exceeds ~70 words (roughly 5 lines of a word-processed document). Earn length through subordinate clauses, participial phrases, and embedded qualifications — not coordination ("and... and... and"). Split sentences that run long on conjunctions.
