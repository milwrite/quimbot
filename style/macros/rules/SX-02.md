# SX-02 — List subject

**Category:** Syntax  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** Yes (all profiles)

---

## Rule

No sentence whose grammatical subject is a list. A sentence opening with "X, Y, Z, and W" almost always has a better singular subject available. Two consecutive list-subject sentences is a hard stop — restructure one or both.

---

## Diagnostic

Does the subject contain a comma and the word "and" or "or" before the verb? If so, find the single governing term, concept, or actor and use that instead.

---

## Examples

**Bad:**
> The ranking algorithm, the data retention pipeline, and the administrative subpoena process all shape participation.

> Frequency, predictability, and contextual cloze success each correlate with reading time.

**Good:**
> These three infrastructure components — ranking, retention, and subpoena — shape participation jointly.

> All three measures correlate with reading time: frequency, predictability, and contextual cloze success.

---

## Fixes

1. Introduce a collective noun or hypernym as subject ("These mechanisms," "All three measures")
2. Restructure so the list becomes the object or a parenthetical
3. Split into parallel sentences with individual subjects

---

## Exception

Lists in subject position are acceptable when the list *is* the point — when enumeration itself carries rhetorical weight. In practice this is rare. When in doubt, restructure.
