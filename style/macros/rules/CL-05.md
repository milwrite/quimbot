# CL-05 — Name the mechanism

**Category:** Claim  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** No (revision habit — required for academic and Reddit-piece profiles)

---

## Rule

Category labels ("infrastructure," "the system," "the platform," "the algorithm") are not mechanisms. Name what actually acts: the ranking algorithm, the data retention pipeline, the administrative subpoena process. If you can't name the mechanism, the claim isn't ready.

---

## Diagnostic

Read every sentence containing "the platform," "the system," "infrastructure," or "the algorithm." Replace each with the specific mechanism. If you can't, the claim needs more work.

---

## Examples

**Bad — category labels:**
> The platform shapes how users participate.

> Infrastructure determines which contributions become visible.

> The algorithm sorts content in ways that benefit certain users.

**Good — named mechanisms:**
> Reddit's Hot algorithm ranks posts by a combination of vote score and recency, which buries contributions that arrive after the early engagement window.

> The data retention pipeline stores deleted posts for 90 days, which means removal is not equivalent to erasure.

> The subpoena compliance process routes law enforcement requests to a dedicated team that responds without user notification.

---

## The term of art exception

When the category label is itself the object of analysis ("the concept of infrastructure"), using the term is legitimate. The ban targets cases where the category label stands in for a specific mechanism the author hasn't named.
