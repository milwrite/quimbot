# DC-02 — Contrastive pivots

**Category:** Diction  
**Added:** 2026-04-06  
**Non-negotiable:** Yes (all profiles)

---

## Rule

Kill "not X but Y", "not just X", "not only X but also Y", "less about X more about Y", "beyond X", "more than X". State the point directly. The contrastive pivot announces that you're about to say something; just say it.

---

## Why

Contrastive constructions defer the real claim. "It's not just about deletion — it's about visibility" contains the real claim in the second half. Write the second half. Cut the first.

---

## Examples

**Bad:**
> The cloze task is not simply a measure of word prediction — it is a measure of contextual recovery.

> This is not about platform policy but about infrastructure.

> The paper argues not only for a new methodology but also for a reconceptualization of reading.

**Good:**
> The cloze task measures contextual recovery, not word prediction.

> The argument concerns infrastructure, not platform policy.

> The paper proposes a new methodology and reconceptualizes what reading models should measure.

---

## The pattern

Every contrastive pivot has a buried direct statement. Find it. Write it. Cut the framing.
