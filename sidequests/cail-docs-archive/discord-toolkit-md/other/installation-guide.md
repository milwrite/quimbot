## Mobile Installation

You can also download the Discord app to your mobile device via iOS or Android. The mobile version gives you immediate access to Discord and can streamline making announcements and replying to students, among other things.

To install the Discord app on your mobile device, simply visit the App Store or Google Play, search for "Discord," and tap "Install." Once installed, open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to sign in or create a new account.
