One swap per month. Assumes 2–3 slots rotate simultaneously on the homepage (verify current slot count with the live site).

| **August** | Looking for Whitman | Natural Sciences OER (LaGuardia) | GC English Students |

| **December** | GC Students of Anthropology | GCDRI | CUNY Academic Commons News |

**Notes on the schedule:**
- AmigAI appears twice (May, November) given its direct connection to CUNY AI Lab work and timeliness for CALI audiences
- NESRI appears twice — high-impact research project with broad institutional visibility
- September rotates in student/reading group sites to coincide with fall semester start
- December closes with the platform itself and its institutional infrastructure
