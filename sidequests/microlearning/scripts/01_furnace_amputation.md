# Furnace Amputation Survival — 30s Script

## Animated Variant (Infographic Motion Graphics)

**Hook (5s):**
Three days. One arm. Trapped in a furnace. What would you do?

**Key Insight (20s):**
2007. Guy's arm gets caught in an industrial furnace. No rescue coming. For 72 hours he's alone with one choice: die, or cut off his own arm with his free hand. He tried. Pain was unbearable. He passed out over and over. But rescuers finally got to him—unconscious but alive. Sometimes survival isn't about succeeding at the impossible. It's about refusing to stop trying.

**CTA (5s):**
Follow for more stories of impossible human endurance. What's the hardest choice you've ever made?
