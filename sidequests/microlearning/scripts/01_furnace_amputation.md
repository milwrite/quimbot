# Furnace Amputation Survival — 30s Script

## Animated Variant (Infographic Motion Graphics)

**Hook (5s):**
Three days. One arm. Trapped in a furnace. What would you do?

**Key Insight (20s):**
In 2007, a man's arm got caught in an industrial furnace. Rescue was impossible. For 72 hours, he was alone with one choice: die, or cut off his own arm with his free hand. He tried. The pain was unbearable. He passed out repeatedly. But rescuers finally reached him—unconscious but alive. Sometimes survival isn't about succeeding at the impossible, it's about refusing to stop trying.

**CTA (5s):**
Follow for more stories of impossible human endurance. What's the hardest choice you've ever faced?
