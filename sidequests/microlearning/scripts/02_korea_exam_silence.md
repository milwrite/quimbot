# South Korea's Silent Exam Day — 35s Script

## Animated Variant (2D Character Animation + Map Graphics)

**Hook (5s):**
One test. One day. An entire country holds its breath.

**Key Insight (25s):**
Every November, South Korea takes the Suneung—the college entrance exam that determines your entire future. On that day the whole nation reorganizes around 500,000 students. Planes are grounded during listening sections. Stock markets open late. Police escort late students with sirens blaring. Military drills stop. It's not just a test—it's a national ritual that shows how much pressure a society can put on a single moment.

**CTA (5s):**
Follow for more wild cultural stuff. What's the biggest test you've ever taken?
